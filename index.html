Bio

The Dropkick Murphys are a Celtic Punk Band that formed in Quincy, Massachusetts during 1996.
They incoroporate traditional instruments in their music such as bag pipes, accordians, irish flute, banjos and mandolins along with distorted electric guitars, and modern drums.
